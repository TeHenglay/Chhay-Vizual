[ 01 ] Philosophy

Architecture is a silent dialogue between the weight of materials and the void of space. My practice focuses on the honesty of concrete and the precision of light.

Based in Phnom Penh, working globally. Specializing in brutalist revivals and contemporary urban interventions that respect historical context while pushing structural limits.

Every line drawn is a commitment to sustainability through longevity. We build not for the decade, but for the century.
